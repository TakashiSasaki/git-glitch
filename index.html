designativeKeyword

This property represents a keyword that directly designates a specific subject. Designative keywords are used to emphasize the essential identity of the subject, independent of its attributes or characteristics. This concept is contrasted with descriptive keywords, and specifies the particular designative keyword for a resource.

descriptiveKeyword

This property represents a keyword that describes a specific attribute, characteristic, or aspect of a subject. Descriptive keywords are used to provide detailed information about the subject, emphasizing its particular features. This concept contrasts with designative keywords, which directly indicate a specific subject.
